I was first introduced to investing like many others. I discovered value investing in the style of Benjamin Grahm and Warren Buffet. It made a lot of sense to me and it was the foundation for my investing framework.
I have learned about other styles however. I admire growth investors such as Peter Lynch, Macro traders such as Ray Dalio, and quantitative investors such as Mark Spitznagel. I do not admire insider trading investors such as Steve Coen.
